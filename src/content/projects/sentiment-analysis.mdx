## Question

How do sentiment models handle messy text? Typos, slang, missing punctuation.

## Method

1. IMDB dataset: 50k reviews
2. Train SVM and BERT on clean data
3. Add noise: typos, word swaps, missing punctuation
4. Measure accuracy drop

## Results

| Data Quality | SVM | BERT |
|-------------|-----|------|
| Clean | 95% | 95% |
| 30% noisy | 78% | 92% |

## Why BERT wins

SVM uses word frequencies. Typo = unknown word = lost signal. BERT uses context. It infers "gret" means "great" from surrounding words.

## Takeaway

User-generated content is messy. Use BERT. Computational cost is worth the accuracy.
